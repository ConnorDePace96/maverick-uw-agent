House rules

Your own underwriting criteria — overlays, hard lines, anything beyond the investor matrix. Every file you run with this on gets explicitly checked against these, and the memo flags them right alongside the investor findings.

House rules are saved to your browser immediately and synced to the cloud store when it's connected (status shown top-right). They apply to every run while the My house rules toggle is on.
